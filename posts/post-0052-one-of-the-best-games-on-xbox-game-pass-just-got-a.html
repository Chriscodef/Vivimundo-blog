One of the Best Games on Xbox Game Pass Just Got a Big Upgrade

Xbox Game Passfans received some great news from indie developer Team Cherry as the originalHollow Knightis now available on Xbox Series X/S and original owners can get the upgrade completely for free.Hollow Knightoriginally launched all the way back in 2017, after a very successful Kickstarter campaign that saw the game cruise past its financial goal, eventually reaching an even bigger audience on theXbox Game Passservice as well. From there, the franchise has become a massive hit, with Team Cherry not only porting the original game to a variety of platforms but making its DLC content completely free for owners as well. Hollow Knight's financial and commercial success led the studio to start work on another entry, which was originally designed to be an expansion but quickly expanded beyond that scope into its own game. After a lengthy development cycle with long periods of total silence,Hollow Knight: Silksonglaunched in 2025 to huge acclaim, winning multiple end-of-year awards and again proving to be a big financial boon for Team Cherry. However, for all that success, Team Cherry is giving fans new and old a big reason to return to the lands of Hallownest. Xbox Game Pass confirms its third day-one release for March 2026, which will arrive in the form of a sequel to a critically acclaimed 2023 title. In a post on social media,Team Cherry announcedthat the originalHollow Knightis available now on next generation consoles, including PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X, and Nintendo Switch 2. The new version takes advantage ofSilksong's various upgrades, giving players a higher resolution and frame-rate, added visual effects, and "additional updates and refinements." Better yet, this update is available completely for free for those who already own the game on a previous platform like the PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox One.
