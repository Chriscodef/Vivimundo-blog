Among these, the Artificer stands out as a master of invention, blending magic with technology to create magical items, consumable items, and more. However, the true diversity of the Artificer class inDungeons and Dragonscomes from its subclasses, each with its own strengths and weaknesses. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just diving into the world of Artificers, subclasses are unlocked inD&Dat level 3, and picking the right one is an important part of building your character. Each subclass can be valued in terms of combat effectiveness, utility (for self and group alike), and versatility, which is why not all subclasses are equal for the Artificer.
