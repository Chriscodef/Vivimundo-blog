Jujutsu Kaisen Modulo Chapter 21 Reveals Dabura' s Domain Expansion

Jujutsu Kaisen ModuloChapter 21 was absolutely massive for the story in many ways. Not only did it drive forward the exciting overarching plot of the story around the optimization of cursed energy, but it also drove forward the overall character arc of Dabura Karaba. As fans know,Dabura is an absolute prodigy when it comes to combat, and his cursed technique is one of the most dangerous ones that fans have seen in the entirety ofJujutsu Kaisen. In JJK Modulo Chapter 21,Dabura has taken this cursed technique to its absolute pinnacle and developed a mighty Domain Expansion. Fans weren't expecting Dabura to create a Domain Expansion so early into the story, but his genius is such that it only took him a matter of chapters to craft such a complex ability and become one of the strongest in the entire world. Dabura is now an absolute monster who can stand shoulder to shoulder with the likes of Gojo and Sukuna, and even Yuji Itadori in the current timeline.His Domain Expansion might just be the most overpowered that the fans have seen in the story. Dabura Karaba is quite easily one of the most talented sorcerers that the fans have seen in the story. Even before Dabura learned how to utilize the various advanced techniques that earthlings utilize with their sorcery, he was an overpowered monster. Even someone as strong as Dura could not stand and combat against him on Simuria, and that was proof of his overwhelming might. For the first time, Dabura Karaba will dance across the battlefield at Sublight speeds. — Narrator Dabura's cursed technique of Murderous Intent and Light is such that he can essentially one-shot the vast majority of the characters. That is precisely why Mahoraga was the perfect opponent for him. In the battle against Mahoraga, Dabura was truly pushed to his limit, and he could even have lost the fight.
