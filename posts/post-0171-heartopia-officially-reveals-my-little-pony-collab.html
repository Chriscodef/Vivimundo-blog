Heartopia Officially Reveals My Little Pony Collab

Por Kevin Ribeiro • 07/02/2026 às 22:28

Heartopiahas officially revealed a collaboration withMy Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, which is due to arrive just in time for Valentine's Day. The cozy life sim has attracted a lot of attention since its launch just over a month ago, and now the special event combiningHeartopiawith theMy Little Ponyfranchise is likely to bring in a whole new group of players. Initially launched on January 7 for mobile devices,Heartopiahad the jump to Steam on January 16, where it has attracted an all-time peak count of more than 64,000 concurrent players. Developed and published by XD, the team behind the free-to-play cozy game describes it as a "multiplayer slow-life simulation," lettingHeartopiafans play with friendsin a relaxed setting that gives them opportunities for activities like fishing, gardening, cooking, bug catching, bird watching, and running through obstacle courses. Though it's only enjoying mixed ratings on Steam, that could be due to its newness, as a lot of players feel it currently works better as a mobile game that still has a lot of room for optimization on PC. The Fallout and My Little Pony franchises are worlds apart, but this highly-rated crossover proves that the two can coexist surprisingly well. Fans of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, an animated show that saw its original run from 2010 to 2019, can soon participate in special events in Heartopia centered around the show. A new announcement from the game's creators, which includes a fresh trailer, shows that the Tree of Harmony will be landing on Whale Island after being magically transported there through a mysterious portal. The ponies will be counting onmembers ofHeartopia's D.G. Guildto master the elements of Harmony, using honesty, kindness, laughter, generosity, loyalty, and magic to restore the iconic tree to its rightful place in Equestria.
