My Dress-Up Darling Season 3 Has Been Delayed Indefinitely

With the first season ofMy Dress-Up Darlingairing back in Winter of 2022, it is safe to say that the anime adaptationquickly gained a lot of popularity. This popularity continued well into the airing of the second season as well, which wrapped up onOctober 5, 2025. The anime revolves around Wakana Gojo and his relationship with Marin Kitagawa. Wakana is interested in becoming a doll-artisan, while Marin regularly enjoys cosplay — which brings the two together.
